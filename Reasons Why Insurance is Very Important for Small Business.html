Small business insurance is often considered unimportant by many small and medium business owners. Since the scale of their business is not that big, they think insurance will only add unnecessary cost which can reduce their profit. But the truth is the opposite. If your small business is not protected by insurance, you are going to be in a big trouble if an accident or a lawsuit happens. These are some reasons why insurance can give your business a big favor.

It Gives You Credibility
Business insurance actually will give you more credibility in front of your customer. To get a business insurance, your business must be registered and licensed, which means you are a legit business. It will make customers trust you and will not hesitate to do business with you.
Small business insurance will not only make you more credible in front of the customers, but also the bank. This is very important just in case you want to borrow some money to develop your business. If your business is not insured, the chance is high that the bank will deny your loan.

It Protects You and Your Employees
If you are running a business where your employees must do some risky jobs, for example like roofing, business insurance is a must to protect your employees. As a matter of fact, insured employee is one of the most important factors a customer look when they want to hire a service. If the employee is not insured and an accident occurred, it is rather you or the customer that needs to pay for the medical expense. That is simply something that every customer will try to avoid in this economy.

It Protects Your Assets
Running a business is not cheap. You need to rent a place, pay employees and many other expenses. The cost you need to spend to run your business will be higher if your business relies to some expensive items. When those items are broken, you need to fix it immediately, otherwise your business will stop running.
Now imagine what’s going to happen to you if you don’t have the means to fix it. If you are unable to run your business, you cannot serve your customers and angry customers equal bad reputation for your business. To avoid this situation, it is essential to purchase a small business insurance plan. It doesn’t have to be the most expensive, just as long as the insurance covers everything you need then everything is settled.
