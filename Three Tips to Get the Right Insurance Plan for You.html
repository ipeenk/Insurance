Choosing the right type of health insurance plan is very important, not only for your health and future, but also your finance. Getting the best insurance policy is definitely the most ideal condition. However, if you have some budget restriction, you really need to think about it thoroughly. The good news is, you can still a good health insurance policy with enough coverage and low price premium as long as you choose it right. Here are some tips to help you choose the best health insurance policy.

Check Your Health and Family History
First of all, it is essential to check your health first before purchasing a policy. Schedule a general checkup to find out your current health condition. The result can give you some supposition regarding which policy you should purchase.
Furthermore, don’t forget to check your family history as well. Even if your medical checkup shows that you are very healthy now, but if you are prone to certain medical condition due to the genetic, it is better to purchase a policy that covers that disease as well. You can prevent yourself from getting the same disease by living healthily. But it is better to be safe than sorry and purchase an insurance with better coverage.

Beware of Out of the Pocket Cost
When shopping for health insurance plan, you definitely will have to choose the network plan. You need to be very thorough when researching this because it can affect the price of the insurance. Some cheaper plan will only allow you to go to health facilities inside the network while some more expensive ones give you flexibility to go anywhere. You shouldn’t jump to the cheapest policy. Instead, you need to consider the out of the pocket cost as well. If the premium rate of the insurance is cheap but you need to spend plenty of out of the pocket cost, you will still be put at disadvantage.

Learn the Quality of the Health Facility in the Network
Before choosing a certain network of the insurance plan, you need to research it first. Find out regarding the health facility of the network. Is it good enough to deal with your health condition? Is the doctor good? Does it have a great specialist? If you are not satisfied with the health facility in the network, you cannot feel the benefit of the health insurance plan you purchase so make sure you pay attention to this.
