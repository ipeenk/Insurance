The Types of Property Insurance
In this case, property insurance in particular home insurance is one of the insurance products that allow in providing financial protection against the home due to a loss caused by fire, criminal acts or damage caused by natural disasters. So, it can guarantee your survival because when a large property such as a home experience something detrimental, in general, you will experience confusion to start again from scratch. Therefore, insurance companies offer several types of insurance for your home in accordance with your economic needs and circumstances, including:

1. Property All Risk. One type of fire insurance that is unnamed perils is where this type of insurance will give you full guarantee against all risks that occur on your property or some other insured interests. However, this insurance does not guarantee property damage caused by several things, such as war, terrorism, business interruption, pollution or contamination, nuclear and radioactive and others. Basically, this type of guarantee provides protection against damage or losses caused by natural disasters such as earthquakes, storms, floods, tsunamis, volcanic eruptions and others. In general, this type of insurance is specifically used to protect property that is non-industrial. Therefore, to protect industrial properties you should choose Industrial All Risk type which has the same policy but only property properties are different.

2. Standard Fire Insurance Policy. It is a type of insurance that also provides protection on your property, especially home financially. However, in this type of insurance, the things that cause losses on your property are severely restricted ie only covers the damage caused by fire, lightning, explosion, aircraft and smoke accidents. Actually, this type of insurance can be converted enable to property all risk but you have to go through some additional procedures
